Elephant Empire

The Cast for November, 2014

November, 2014. Elephant Empire will be performing live at the Bad Dog Theatre every Monday night (Facebook Event) with their new show, The Pirate Twins.

Mark Little will be joining the cast of Elephant Empire for the first time in recorded history. This episode also stars Andrew Gardner, Peter Stevens, and Matt Lemche.

The first half of the show will feature sketch comedy with our new original tale. The second half will be improv.

In 2007, Capitol Records took Jammie Thomas-Rasset to court and won over $222,000 for 24 songs that were downloaded on her computer. Canada has yet to see a file-sharing copyright infringement lawsuit against an individual.

In Elephant Empire's November show, The Pirate Twins, it happens.
