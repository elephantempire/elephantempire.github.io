We're a comedy troupe in Toronto.

An Elephant Empire show features an original story told in three parts. Expect strange, dramatic, and with heart. We get tense with comic relief. The rest of the show is sprinkled with fast and silly sketch comedy.

Here's an entire half hour Elephant Empire show in a ninety second teaser.

"I like the dramatic tone that's sprinkled in the comedy. It's cool to feel something and laugh at the same time. THERE I SAID IT!!"

-Danny B.

This teaser shows our two comedy gears:
1) comic relief within the story
2) fast 'n silly.

Remember the Dibigong

A group of friends spend a fateful night on a little boat looking for the legendary Dibigong of Lake Ontario. Their friendships are destroyed because they cannot agree what happened that night.

What Charlie Would Have Wanted

Charlie's dead and his will had an unusual burial request. But was it a joke? The people closest to him must decide. Are they prepared to steal the body from the family's funeral to "throw it off a cliff"?
