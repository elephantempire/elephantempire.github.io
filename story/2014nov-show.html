The Fate of the Pirate Twins is about two friends excited to start their "big lives" now that university is done... until a devastating lawsuit for internet piracy falls on one of their heads.

Also in this show: bungee wedding, honking works, Chet Sucrette's nightmares, 'did you say Roboclop?' and more!
